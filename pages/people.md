Using [LEAP](https://qcbs.ca/qcbs-research-showcase?showcase_id=35) at McGill Gault Nature Reserve, we are studying the evolutionary responses of bacteria after herbicide contamination.<br>Background: I completed my B.Sc. at McGill University in Microbiology and Immunology.
